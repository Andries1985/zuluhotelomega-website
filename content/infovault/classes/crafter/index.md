## Skills
- Arms lore
- Blacksmithy
- Bowcraft/Fletching
- Carpentry
- Lumberjacking
- Mining
- Tailoring
- Tinkering

## Bonuses
- Bonuses in all their specialized skills.
- Gains strength quicker.

## Penalties
- Can only cast circle 1 spells.
- Can’t wear strength mods.
- Gains intelligence slower.
