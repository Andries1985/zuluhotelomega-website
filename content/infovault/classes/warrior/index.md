## Skills
- Anatomy
- Fencing
- Healing
- Mace Fighting
- Parrying
- Swordsmanship
- Tactics
- Wrestling

## Bonuses
- Bonuses in all their specialized skills.
- Deals increased damage with melee weapons.
- Receives less melee damage.
- Gains strength quicker.

## Penalties
- Can’t equip intelligence mods.
- Takes increased spell damage.
- Deals less spell damage.
- Cast spells slower.
- Won’t get any bonuses from equipping magic reflect items.
- Can only cast circle 1 spells.
- Gains intelligence slower.
