## Skills
- Begging
- Cartography
- Discordance
- Herding
- Musicianship
- Peacemaking
- Provocation
- Taste Identification

## Bonuses
- Bonuses in all their specialized skills.
- Can use Book of Songs.
- Can use Archaic book of Verses
- Gains dexterity and intelligence quicker.
- Can use shepherd’s crook without mace-fighting, damage and hit chance will check for class level instead.

## Penalties
- Can’t wear plate armor.
- Can’t wear shields.
- Can only cast circle 1 spells.
- Gains strength slower.

## Additional info
To fill up the Book of Songs you will need to buy Pen and Ink (Mapmaker NPC) and simply double-click it. Then target the song scroll and the Book of Songs you wish to scribe the song into.
To fill up the Archaic book of Verses you need to use the verse scroll by double-clicking it. Upon success it will automatically be added to the Verse book.
