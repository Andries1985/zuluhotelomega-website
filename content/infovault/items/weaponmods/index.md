## Stat prefixes
When an item receives a negative modifier it means the item is cursed. 

Cursed items cannot be unequipped. Visit a High Priest to uncurse the item. Simply say "Uncurse" to be able to unequip it or "Purify" to revert the item to it's positive form. 

For example a purified Frail (-8 str) will turn into a Hero's (+8 str) modifier.
