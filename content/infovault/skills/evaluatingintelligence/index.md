Using evaluating intelligence on a player or npc will determine their intelligence, protections and current mana.

If your evaluating intelligence skill is higher than 90 it will show the percentage of mana the target has left with the message:
- This being is at XXX% mental strength.
- Evaluating intelligence can also show protections of npcs, this will require at least 135 for normal bosses and 150 for super bosses.
- Evaluating intelligence will also increase your damage and duration of spells.

| Intelligence | Message                                                          |
|--------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 0-15         | Slightly less intelligent than a rock.                           |
| 16-30        | Fairly Stupid.                                                   |
| 31-45        | Not the brightest.                                               |
| 46-60        | About Average.                                                   |
| 61-75        | Moderately intelligent.                                          |
| 76-90        | Very intelligent.                                                |
| 91-105       | Extremely intelligent.                                           |
| 106-120      | Extraordinarily intelligent.                                     |
| 121-135      | Like a formidable intellect, well beyond even the extraordinary. |
| 136-150      | Like a definite genius.                                          |
| Above 150    | Superhumanly intelligent in a manner you cannot comprehend.      |
