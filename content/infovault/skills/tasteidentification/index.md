Using taste identification on a poisonous potion/food will try to determine the poison level on the item.

You can also directly try to drink/eat the poisonous item to try to determine it's poison level.

| Poison lvl         | Difficulty | Message                     |
|--------------------|------------|-----------------------------|
| 1                  | 15         | a lesser poison.            |
| 2                  | 30         | a minor poison.             |
| 3                  | 45         | a regular poison.           |
| 4                  | 60         | a strong poison.            |
| 5                  | 75         | a greater poison.           |
| 6                  | 90         | a deadly poison.            |
| "10" psn + suffix  | 150        | an extremely deadly poison. |
