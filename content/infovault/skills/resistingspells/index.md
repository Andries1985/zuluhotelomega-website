This skill is checked automatically each time you are attacked by magic.

If successful you take less damage from the spell.

You will gain higher resisting spells the higher circle the spell casted on you is, however you need to resist it to be able to gain.

Higher resisting spells and specialized mage class will make it easier to resist spells and lower the damage even more from spells.
