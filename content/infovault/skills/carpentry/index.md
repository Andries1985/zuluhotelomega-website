To craft or repair something with carpentry you will need to use one of the carpentry tools: Saw, Dovetail saw, Draw knife, Froe, Inshave, Scorp, Jointing plane, Moulding plane, Smoothing plane or Hammer.

Using a carpentry tool on wood equipment near an anvil will try to repair it, higher carpentry and arms lore will increase your chance of repairing the item.

To craft items with multiple materials you will need to use your carpentry tool on the secondary material first and then the logs.

Being higher specialized crafter classe and have higher carpentry will increase the crafted item quality and chance of being exceptional.
