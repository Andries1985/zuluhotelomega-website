You can use mining to either mine ore or to dig for sand.

To mine ore you need to double-click a pickaxe or shovel then select a cave floor to mine from.

To dig for sand you will need to use a shovel and target sand.

Higher mining skill and specialized crafter class will generate more ores, mining with Omero’s pickaxe will double the amount of ores you get.

>Standing outside of guarded/safe zones will give you higher chance on getting zulu ores and gems aswell as generate more of all kind of ores/gems.

Having at least 90 mining and being specialized crafter class will make you handle the pickaxe/shovel so good that it won’t break.
