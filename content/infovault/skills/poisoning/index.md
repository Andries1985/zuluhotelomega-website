You can use poisoning to poison either weapons, food or bandages.

Poisoning weapons will make it more difficult depending on the damage mod on the weapon.

>Poisoned bandages can be used by specialized thiefs to heal.

Poisoned weapons made with the poisoning skill will only poison a target if the attacker is a specialized thief.

## Poisoning Difficulties

| Poison level | Skill required |
|--------------|----------------|
| 1            | 20             |
| 2            | 40             |
| 3            | 60             |
| 4            | 80             |
| 5            | 100            |
| 6            | 120            |
