To scribe scrolls from books you will need to first target blank scrolls then the book you want to scribe from.

To scribe scrolls to books you will need to first target the scroll then the book you want to inscribe it to.

To copy a spellbook you need to target the book you want to copy first then an empty book.

To make a runebook you need to have all the ingredients in your backpack and target an unmarked rune.
