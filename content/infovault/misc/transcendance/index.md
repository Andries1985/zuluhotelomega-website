When double-clicking a transcendence scroll you will be prompted by the gump above.

By clicking on any of the checkboxes you will immediately improve that skill. Be warned there is no confirmation box.

The higher the skill the less skill improvements you will get from a transcendence scroll.

Amount:
- 0-49.9 = 20 skill points
- 50-74.9 = 15 skill points
- 75-89.9 = 10 skill points
- 90-104.9 = 5 skill points
- 105-119.9 = 2 skill points
- 120-134.9 = 1 skill point
- 135-144.9 = 0.5 skill point
- 145 and up = 0.1 skill point
