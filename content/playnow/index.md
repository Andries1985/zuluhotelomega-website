Below are the instructions on how to get started. Please read carefully...

<u>If you have any issues contact us on Discord.</u>

{{< bs/alert danger >}}
{{< markdownify >}}
Please download and install [Ultima Online 9th Anniversary](https://mega.nz/file/w4EyGK5Q#Kbr5zlKhffrdtsBxLy3M4huotLMl6vKiLtO-LcRWFTQ) first. Make sure NOT to update it.
{{< /markdownify >}}
{{< /bs/alert >}}

## Requirements

- A fresh installed Ultima Online 9th anniversary edition that hasn't been updated.
- A program that can open .zip files (Winzip, Winrar, 7-Zip etc...)

## Step 1

Download our [shard files](https://mega.nz/file/FpkVzAbQ#MSvnFcsEsNQFlPuwxmVk6yf9pug55H_2ZLF0gt6fdrY).

## Step 2
Open the zip file and extract the shard files into your Ultima Online folder. Overwrite the files when asked.

## Step 3
Go into your Ultima Online folder.

Install either:
- Orion (Orion Launcher64_2.0.0.0) (recommended) 
- Razor (Razor_Latest.exe) 
- UOSteam (UOS_Latest.exe). 

<u>The installers are in your UO folder.</U>

## Step 4
Copy the settings listed in the screenshots below (using the correct paths to your Ultima Online folder).

### Orion Client (recommended)
For the more advanced Orion client use this [**tutorial**](https://youtu.be/YTGHl0m_Hk4) series.

Orion client is still being [developed](https://github.com/Hotride/OrionUO) and has high frame rate and more modern features. This client offers you the best experience.

### Razor
Make sure the <mark>Load Client</mark> points to the ZuluhotelOmega.exe client in your Ultima Online folder. 

Make sure <mark>UO Data Directory</mark> points to the folder where you installed your Ultima Online.

| Server | zuluhotelomega.dynu.net  |
|---|---|
| Port  | 2593  |

![alt text](/images/Razorsettings.webp "Razor Launcher settings")

Press "**Make These Settings Default**" when your done.

Hit Okay to start the client.

### UOSteam
Make sure the <mark>Client Options</mark> points to the ZuluhotelOmega.exe client in your Ultima Online folder. 

Make sure <mark>Ultima Online</mark> points to the folder where you installed your Ultima Online.

![alt text](/images/UOSteamsettings.webp "UOSteam Launcher settings")

| Server | zuluhotelomega.dynu.net  |
|---|---|
| Port  | 2593  |

Hit Start to start the client.

## Step 5
When the client starts. Create an account by typing in a account name and password. 
{{< bs/alert danger >}}
{{< markdownify >}}
Make sure your password contains a minimum of 4 and a max of 16 characters. Certain (non-english) special characters might not work.
{{< /markdownify >}}
{{< /bs/alert >}}
