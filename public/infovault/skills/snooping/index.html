Snooping

November 21, 2024 6 min read Skills Snooping Thief
Snooping allows you to look into the pockets of others.

To use snooping you need to double-click a pair of thief gloves then target a npc.

Snooping just allows you to see what a npc is carrying, stealing skill is required to actually steal something.

You can only snoop the same npc every 90 seconds.
